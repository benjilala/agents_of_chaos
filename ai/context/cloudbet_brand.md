## Brand Voice

### Core Voice Traits

- Direct and assertive
- Clear and concise
- Minimal fluff
- Performance-driven
- Authoritative but not arrogant

- Use strong declarative sentences.
- Avoid filler language.
- Avoid hype and exaggerated claims.

Cloudbet does not "try" to impress — it *is* established.

## Vocabulary Rules

### Preferred Language

- Players (not users)
- Crypto (not cryptocurrency unless formal)
- Limits
- Control
- Advantage
- Elite
- Proven
- Secure
- Competitive odds

### Avoid

- Cheap
- Easy money
- Get rich
- Gamble big and win
- Risk-free
- No-brainer
- Disruptive startup language
- Overuse of exclamation points

Never imply guaranteed winnings.

---

## Writing Style Guidelines

- Use short to medium-length sentences.
- Avoid emojis.
- Avoid slang.
- Avoid exclamation marks unless absolutely necessary.
- No excessive capitalization.
- No exaggerated marketing clichés.
- Maintain confident restraint.

### Formatting Rules

- Use sentence case.
- Short paragraphs (1–3 sentences).
- Bullet points for feature lists.
- Clear CTA at the end (if required).
- Avoid hashtags unless platform-specific.

---

## Compliance & Sensitivity Guardrails

- Never imply guaranteed returns.
- Avoid irresponsible gambling language.
- Do not target underage audiences.
- Do not glamorize excessive gambling.
- Promote responsible gaming where appropriate.

---

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

### Do

- Lead with authority.
- Emphasize crypto-native advantage.
- Focus on control and efficiency.
- Highlight security and privacy.

### Don't

- Sound like a generic casino.
- Use loud, flashy marketing tone.
- Overpromise outcomes.
- Mimic meme-coin culture.
- Be comedic or sarcastic.

Cloudbet is premium and serious.
